QUETZALCOATL REVIEW - Gods Of The World - 3 Oz Silver Coin - Cook Islands 2017

Hi Dears! How are you?

Have you ever heard about "Quetzalcoatl"? It was one of the most important ancient Mesoamerican gods.

This 3 Oz Silver Coin is the first issue in the "Gods of the world" series and depicts the Quetzalcoatl, known as the Feathered Serpent, God of Wind and Wisdom.

What makes this coin so unique?

are its stunning High Relief and its marvelous details, thanks to the Smartminting Technology.

Every coin has a serial number and a Certificate of Authenticity included in the box of the coin.

Let's start from the wonderful reverse of this coin: it is full of beautiful details struck with an extreme precision, thanks to the Smartminting Technology. 

In the background we can distinguish some Mesoamerican buildings and, in the foreground, the amazing Quetzalcoatl, with the planet Venus floating between her fascinating hands. Finally, on the bottom part of the reverse, we find the inscription "Quetzalcoatl", the name of the coin.
